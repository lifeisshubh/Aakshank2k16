About Us

Aakshank is a annual cutural festival of Institute of Engineering and Technology, DAVV which was started by a group of enthusuastic IETians, in 2001, in order to provide a grooming stage to the budding talents in the Institute, Aakshank has transformed itself into the most zestful and extra-ordinary cultural bonanza in Central India. The event cover a wide array of events ranging from competition in varied genre like Dance, Drama, Singing to Star Night, DJ Night, StuntMania and plenty of informals.
